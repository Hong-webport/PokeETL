Team Triangle Project Page

What in the world is all of this?

Hopefully this will help shed some light on any confusion if you stumbled upon our project’s repo page and can’t figure out what is going on.
Team Triangle is a team of students currently enrolled in the Georgia Tech Data Science program. Unlike most sessions in the program, this entire class has happened remotely due to some kind of pandemic. Our four-person team (named after a three-sided object) was chosen by the instructors with an initial deadline of just under 2 weeks to complete. The project(s) on the Git Page were planned, put together, and hosted via Zoom meetings and lots of coffee.
The final part of this project is a Zoom presentation detailing our work.

Wish us luck!
